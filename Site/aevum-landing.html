QUESTIONS

Frequently asked.

What data does Aevum collect?

+

None. Aevum stores your date of birth locally in your browser's storage. No analytics. No tracking. No servers. The code is fully open source for you to verify.

Is it really free?

+

Completely free. No premium tier, no subscription, no upsells. If you want to support the project, you can buy a coffee — but it's entirely optional.

Why does the extension need the "storage" permission?

+

To save your date of birth and theme preferences locally. That's the only permission. No network requests, no remote servers, no data leaves your machine.

Can I customize the life expectancy?

+

The default is 80 years (global average). You can change it in settings. The grid adapts instantly.

Does it affect browser performance?

+

Aevum is ~49KB total. The physics engine runs at 60fps only when visible. It has zero impact on your browsing experience.
